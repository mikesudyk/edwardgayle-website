Low Tech by Design

Your fridge doesn't need WiFi or AI. Your mother's fridge didn't have a motherboard, so why should yours? We use mechanical switches and sensors, and there are no updates to install.

Compressors that won't quit. Metal parts, not plastic. A rock solid frame and serious insulation. Made only to keep your food cold and to work for decades.

Our Story

Inspired by the Golden Age. Built for the next one.

American manufacturers used to build refrigerators with pride, fridges that families kept for generations. Simple compressors, mechanical thermostats, heavy-gauge steel. They hummed along without a single thought of a service call.

Then came "efficiency" requirements, and after that came leaning out manufacturing processes to boost margins. If that weren't enough, they added WiFi, a screen, and then AI despite no one asking for any of it.

We believe your fridge should just work. That's it.
